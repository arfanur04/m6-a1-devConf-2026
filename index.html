FAQ

What is this developer conference about?

This conference brings together developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts to learn about the latest trends, tools, and best practices in software development.

Who can attend the conference?

Anyone interested in technology can attend! Whether you are a beginner, student, or experienced professional, you are welcome.

Do I need prior coding experience?

No prior experience is required. There will be beginner-friendly sessions as well as advanced talks for experienced developers.

How can I register?

You can register through our official website by filling out the registration form and completing the payment process.

Will sessions be recorded?

Yes, most sessions will be recorded and shared with registered participants after the event.
